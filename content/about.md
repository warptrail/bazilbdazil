## SECTION OVERVIEW

This section is not meant to function as a traditional biography.

It should feel atmospheric, theatrical, layered, mysterious, intimate, and emotionally human without becoming overly verbose or overly mystical.

The experience should feel like:
- opening a hidden dossier
- stepping backstage after the performance
- uncovering fragments of a cosmic transmission
- entering a quieter chamber of the website

The tone balance is important:
- theatrical but grounded
- mystical but self-aware
- playful but emotionally sincere
- stylish but readable
- immersive but not cluttered

Avoid:
- corporate "About Me" language
- long autobiographical paragraphs
- generic wellness spirituality
- cliché tarot tropes
- fake ancient mystic language
- excessive exposition

The section should support:
- parallax layers
- reveal animations
- expandable/collapsible behavior
- floating visual fragments
- large typography moments
- atmospheric transitions
